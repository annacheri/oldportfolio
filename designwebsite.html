Sentier Strategic Resources Web Redesign

Brief

Web design and front-end development freelance project to rebrand company website. Work began in December 2017 and ended April 2018. I worked closely with the Sentier team to take their already established brand strategy and reboot its visual identity, shaping their tone and voice, and developing a new website.

Visit Website

My Role

  • Implemented Hugo Static Site Generator while using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery to achieve project goals
  • Designed for web, mobile, and iPad screens
  • Worked independently and on occasion remotely with UX designers
  • Managed project development, timeline, and client communication

Solution

Our goal was to make a website that could be handed off to designers and marketers that was modular enough to be expanded by the company in the future without outside development help. With Hugo Static Site Generator, the blog posting feature is much more editable and dynamic, allowing writers to edit text in markdown and focus their attention on content rather than layout.
